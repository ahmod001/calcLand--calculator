Area calculator

How it works

  • To convert a square meter measurement to an acre measurement, multiply the area by the conversion ratio. Since one square meter is equal to 0.000247 acres, formula is:
    acres = square meters × 0.000247
  • To convert a hector to acre measurement, multiply input by the conversion ratio. formula:
    Acre = Hectare X 2.471051565

How to use it

  • Enter the size of area.
  • Click on the side of input field to change the measurement unit. The default unit is square-meter (m²).
  • Click the "measure" button to convert the entered area size to acres.
  • To copy the result, click the "copy" button.
